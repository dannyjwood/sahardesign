Brand Style Guide, Strutta

As the design team grew and the brand matured, our team at Strutta felt the pressure to improve consistency and up-level the website. My role was to find a solution to these growing pains.

To improve consistency, I worked with our product lead to create a design system that streamlined the current brand assets and common UI elements.

It was important that the design system was flexible enough to grow and develop with the brand. My solution was to create a living style guide where designers and developers could access and edit the design system.
